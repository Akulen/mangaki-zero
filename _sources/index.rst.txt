Welcome to Mangaki Zero's documentation!
========================================

.. image:: https://github.com/mangaki/zero/workflows/CI/badge.svg
   :alt: Mangaki Zero's CI status!
   :target: https://github.com/mangaki/zero/actions

.. image:: https://codecov.io/gh/mangaki/zero/branch/master/graph/badge.svg
   :alt: Mangaki Zero's code coverage
   :target: https://codecov.io/gh/mangaki/zero

Mangaki's recommendation algorithms. They power the website https://mangaki.fr and can be used for benchmarking on other datasets such as Movielens.

They are tested on Python 3.6, 3.7, 3.8 over OpenBLAS LP64 & MKL.
